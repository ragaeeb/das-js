ASH-SHAYKH ABU 'ABDULLAH MAHIR IBN DHAFIR AL-QAHTANI

LIVE IN PERSON
IN OTTAWA, ONTARIO, CANADA...

He is a Khateeb in Jami’ ul Bilaal in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia.
